    Book

    This is a book that I love to read every time I have free time. “Nha Gia Kim” is a book by author Paulo Coelho. The story is about Santiago, a Spanish shepherd, who decides to pursue his dream after meeting an alchemist. The book is Santiago's journey to find treasure, but more than that, it is also a journey of self-discovery and a better understanding of life. “The Alchemist” is an inspirational story about following your dreams and following your heart.
